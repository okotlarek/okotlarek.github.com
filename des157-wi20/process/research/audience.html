User Research - Personas

Because my interaction will differ based on the political knowledge and involvement of the user, I have crafted these general personas on a spectrum of political involvement. In order, they are Politically Withdrawn, Politically Ambivalent, Politically Casual, and Politically Active.

Withdrawn

  • Little to no faith in the government or the political system.
  • Thinks "my vote doesn't count".
  • May have some guiding principles that inform political opinions but is not politically active.

Ambivalent

  • Doesn't think politics is useful or interesting.
  • May think their vote doesn't count.
  • Doesn't like to discuss politics with friends or family.
  • Generally unaware of stances that political parties hold on some issues.

Casual

  • Knows where parties align on certain issues, particularly the "big" ones (abortion, guns, health care, etc).
  • Will vote in presidential elections, less likely to vote in primary, midterm, and local elections.
  • More likely to argue against ideas that conflict with their political party's beliefs than to advocate for their own beliefs.
  • May not hold strong political beliefs.

Active

  • Holds strong political beliefs. Not likely to be swayed.
  • Will vote in primary, local, midterm, and presidential elections.
  • Spends more (or equal) time advocating for their platform than arguing against opposing beliefs.
